# DATA LICENSE AND ATTRIBUTION

## Dataset Name  
**NYC Department of Education – School Quality Reports: High School Results (2024–2025)**



## Data Provider  
New York City Department of Education (NYC DOE)



## Source and Access  
This project uses publicly available data published by the New York City Department of Education as part of the **School Quality Reports (SQR)** for the 2024–2025 academic year. The data and accompanying documentation are made available through official NYC DOE publications and NYC Open Data portals.

## Derivative Works and Transformations  

This repository contains **derived datasets and analytical outputs** created from the original NYC DOE School Quality Reports data. These include, but are not limited to:

- Computation of composite indicators (e.g., Readiness Gap)  
- Statistical modeling outputs and residual-based analyses  
- Aggregations and disaggregations by school and demographic subgroup  

These derivative works are **independent scholarly products** and do not represent official NYC DOE statistics or interpretations.

Unless otherwise stated, all derivative data and analytical code in this repository are released under the same open-use principles as the original data source, consistent with the NYC Open Data Terms of Use.



## Privacy and Ethical Use  

The source data are aggregated at the school level and released by NYC DOE in compliance with student privacy protections, including minimum cell-size suppression rules.

This project does not contain personally identifiable information (PII) and does not attempt to identify or infer outcomes for individual students. Users of this repository are expected to adhere to the same ethical standards when using or extending this work.
